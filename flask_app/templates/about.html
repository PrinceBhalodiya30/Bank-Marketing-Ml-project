Random Forest Ensemble

Deposit Insight utilizes a Random Forest Classifier, an industry-standard ensemble learning method. By constructing a multitude of decision trees during training and outputting the consensus class, we eliminate the common pitfalls of overfitting associated with single decision trees. This results in a robust, generalized model capable of accurate predictions on unseen market data.
